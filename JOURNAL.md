# 3/22/2026 7 AM - Routing

_Time spent: 4.5h_

I did routing today. I made sure the traces of 3.3v and 5v were at least 0.3mm, and the main trace was 0.6 mm. It took a lot of time while routing the tps63070  due to an improper footprint, but somehow I finally achieved it. The traces from the SD card to the MCU became too long. I added ground power planes on both layers (the top and bottom copper layers). I wanted to make a 4-layer PCB, but to make the PCB a bit cheaper, I did a 2-layer PCB. I kept the rest of the traces 0.2mm. But the traces from decoupling capacitors were 0.3mm. I took a lot of time, so I did 2 hours the previous day and another 2.5 hours this day. Although the routing of the 2-layer PCB was tough for some of the SPI lines, I used the autorouter in EASYEDA. I faced a lot of problems at the end of PCB while doing DRC. At first, it showed so many errors, but later I managed to correct all the errors I made while routing.
